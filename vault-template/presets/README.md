# Presets — operating lenses (examples)

A **preset** is a selectable operating lens — it biases focus, default domains, tone, and
which instruments lead, **without changing the knowledge, identity, or laws beneath it**
(the authority is [`PERSONA-PRESETS.md`](../../PERSONA-PRESETS.md); switch with `/preset`).

The two presets shipped here — `gtm-lead` and `ai-orchestrator` — are **EXAMPLES**,
modelled on one owner's dual role. They are **owner-defined**: at instantiation (`/setup`)
you keep, edit, or replace them, and author your own from
[`_templates/preset.md`](../_templates/preset.md) for your roles. Set the starting lens via
`active_preset` in [`memory/core.md`](../memory/core.md).
